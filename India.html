India TV is next on list of top 10 news channel in India. It is an India's popular Hindi news station situated in Noida, Uttar Pradesh. They founded Independent News Service (INS), the main business of India TV, in 1997. In April 2004, he and his wife co-founded India TV from a facility in Film City, Noida. Its Broadcast Center is located in Sector 85, Noida, Uttar Pradesh, India. Rajat Sharma earlier presented Aap Ki Adalat on Zee News and other program that he hosted was Janata Ki Adalat on Star News before founding India TV. Aap Ki Adalat, India's oldest continuously telecasted television program, it will celebrate its 28th anniversary in 2021.
